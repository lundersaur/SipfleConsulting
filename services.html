Sipfle Consulting provides a broad range of consulting services for nonprofit organizations. Past clients and the team’s experience includes working with nonprofit organizations with a range of activities and missions.

We look forward to working with you to achieve your goals.

Strategic Services and Capacity Building for Nonprofits

Sipfle Consulting offers a range of services designed to guide the strategic direction of nonprofits and enhance capacity as they work towards their mission. This includes strategic planning, capitalization planning, completing market studies or other research projects, and supporting other organizational needs.

Grant Writing

Sipfle Consulting’s team has raised more than $40 million in grant funding and offers comprehensive grant writing services as well grant application review and support. Sipfle Consulting specializes in the CDFI Fund’s grant programs including Financial Assistance and Technical Assistance applications within the CDFI and NACA program, Bank Enterprise Award (BEA), Small Dollar Loan Program, and the Capital Magnet Fund (CMF). Sipfle Consulting also completes grants for foundations and other government entities.

Sipfle Consulting also completes compliance reporting for select CDFI Fund programs and foundation grants.

CDFI Services

Sipfle Consulting helps organizations to understand and apply the CDFI Fund’s certification requirements, whether they are considering certification for the first time, are interested in monitoring their certification standing on an ongoing basis, or are completing a recertification application.

CDFI Services include Annual Certification Reports and other CDFI Fund reporting.

Research, Impact and Data

Let us help you think through your data challenges and questions. Projects include impact evaluation creating impact reports; assessing data collection and utilization systems; leveraging existing data to guide decision making; and research projects to answer organization or market specific questions.
