Sipfle Consulting is led by Emily Sipfle. Emily has 15 years of experience in the CDFI industry. She started her professional career working at three Chicago-based CDFIs and since has worked with dozens of CDFIs nationally in consulting in research roles.

Most recently she was the executive director of Fund Community Institute, a research organization focused on the CDFI industry. She has a passion for community development and supporting nonprofits as they achieve their missions.
